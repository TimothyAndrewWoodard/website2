The basic principals beginner chess players should learn are as follows, in sequential order


Basic Checkmates

The first thing any chess player needs to master after learning the rules of the game, are the basic checkamtes. The most basic is King and Queen versus King. After that comes King and Rook versus King.


More advanced checkmates

Sometimes a player has drawing possibilities by trading off his pieces for your pawns. So learnging how to checkmate with two bishops or two knights can be helpful


Basic King and Pawn endings
